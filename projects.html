Projects

Some might call it hobbies, but I like to think of them as my little projects. Because I approach many of my hobbies with an idea or a goal in mind. Whether it is creating this website from scratch, a personal best in the gym, or the perfection of my loaf of bread. A completion of such a little "project" makes me happy and allows me to pursue another of my countless hobbies.

In the future I might post specific projects and things I've created here, but for the time being it is merely a collection of a few selected hobbies and interests of mine.

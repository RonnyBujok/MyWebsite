My name is Ronny Bujok (IPA: /ˈbu.jɔk/), I was born on 13th April 1994 and I am currently living in the Netherlands and doing a PhD.

Ever since I remember I have always been curious, easily fascinated by anything and always looking to learn. Even if I cannot do something, I will at least give it a try. My passion to learn and discover is reflected by my many and varied hobbies, but also in my decision to pursue a career in research.
